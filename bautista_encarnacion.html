PLAYOFF MEMORIES (2015-2017)

  1. From 2015-2017, the Jays made the playoffs twice
  2. Bautista and Encarnacion cemented their postseason status during this period. Some memories include:
    1. Bautista 3-run Home Run in Game 5 of the ALDS (Division Series) in 2015 to give them a 6-3 lead over Texas and eventually win the series
    2. Encarnacion walk-off Home Run in the Wild Card game in 2016 Vs. Baltimore
    3. Bautista Game-Tying 2-Run Home Run in Game 6 of the ALCS in 2015 to (potentially) save their season

LEAVE AND RETIREMENT (2017-2020)

  1. Jays don't make the playoffs again in the 2010s
  2. Encarnacion and Bautista go to different teams:
    1. Encarnacion: Cleveland Indians
    2. Bautista: Atlanta Braves
