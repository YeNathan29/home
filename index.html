TORONTO BLUE JAYS:

BACKGROUND INFORMATION:

  1. PROFESSIONAL BASEBALL TEAM
    • Located in Toronto, Ontario
      1. More Specifically Downtown Toronto
      2. Intersection between Blue Jays Way and Bremner Blvd
  2. League: MLB
  3. Home Stadium/Ballpark: Rogers Centre
    1. Was originated in 1989
    3. Original home ballpark: Exhibition Stadium (until 1989)
    4. Spring Training (PreSeason) Ballpark: TD Ballpark
  4. Founded in 1977
  5. FUN FACT: CURRENTLY THE ONLY TEAM NORTH OF THE BORDER (CANADA) IN MLB!
