  1. Right Behind Level 100

      • Seat Section TicketMaster Lounge (Home Plate)

      • Seat Sections #335-345 + CONTROL ROOM (Third Base to Home Plate)

LEVEL 500 (FINAL LEVEL)

  1. Highest Seats in the Ballpark
  2. Cheapest Seats
  3. Includes Corona Rooftop Patio
  4. Park Social is included
  5. Does include Hotels
  6. 5 main Sections:
    1. Outfield
      • Seat Sections #508 + CORONA ROOFTOP PATIO (Right Field)
      • Seat Sections #539-540 + PARK SOCIAL (Left Field)
    2. Infield (1st and 3rd Base) and Foul Lines
      • Seat Sections #509-516 (Right Field Foul Line)
      • Seat Sections #517-523 (First Base to Home Plate)
      • Seat Sections #524-525 (Home Plate)
      • Seat Sections #526-532 (Third Base to Home Plate)
      • Seat Sections #533-538 (Left Field Foul Line)
    3. CORONA ROOFTOP PATIO
      • No Seating Whatsoever
      • Newly Created Area, came during the recent Renovation
      • Includes new restaurants, shops, and a patio for dining
    4. PARK SOCIAL
      • Included for Mental Health Awareness
      • Area of Socialization
      • No seating
    5. HOTELS (Center Field)
      • Budweiser Hotels at Rogers Centre
      • There is a window to watch the game from the Hotel
